# Spec: M1.4 Contracts and Digital Signatures

## Problem Statement

Accepted estimates must become enforceable contracts and change orders must be signable before affecting escrow, payments or project execution.

## Scope

- In scope:
  - Dropbox Sign / HelloSign client abstraction.
  - Contract templates by trade.
  - Estimate-to-contract generation.
  - Embedded signing URLs.
  - Contract signed state feeding escrow activation.
- Out of scope:
  - Legal advice generation.
  - Jurisdiction-specific lien waiver templates; see M2.1.

## Contract

```yaml
contract:
  source: "estimate | change_order"
  status: "draft | sent | partially_signed | fully_executed | voided"
  provider: "hellosign | mock"
  embeddedSigningUrl: "optional"
effects:
  fully_executed:
    - "escrow can become active if payment conditions are met"
forbidden_behavior:
  - "activate escrow from unsigned required contract"
```
